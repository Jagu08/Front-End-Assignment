About THC

The Helicopter company saw light in 2018 in Riyadh as a unique endeavor conceived by the Public Investment Fund (PIF), offering commercial Helicopter transportation services to ultimately enhance the tourism and business environment in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. The circumstances that led to the creation of the Helicopter Company emerged from the need for development of operations and services to offer reliable, ground-breaking transport solutions.
